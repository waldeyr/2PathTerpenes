Interactive Derivation Hypergraph

Explore the chemical derivation network as an interactive "metro map": molecules are stations, and reactions (hyperedges, with possibly multiple reactants and products) are the connections between lines. Click a station for molecule details, click a connection for the reaction rule(s) applied, and use the controls below to search and filter.
